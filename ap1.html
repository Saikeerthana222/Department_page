Applications of AI & Data Science

AI & Data Science are transforming industries worldwide. Below are key applications in medicine, finance, and engineering.

AI in Medicine

AI helps in early disease detection, drug discovery, and robotic surgeries. Machine learning models analyze medical data for accurate diagnostics.

AI in Finance

AI-driven systems detect fraud, automate trading, and optimize investments by analyzing financial data patterns in real time.

AI in Engineering

AI enhances automation, optimizes manufacturing processes, and enables predictive maintenance in various engineering fields.
